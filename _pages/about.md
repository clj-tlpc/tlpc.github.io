## Who are the Lonesome Poets?
Have you ever felt alone surrounded by friends? A feeling of being watched when no one is around? Completely lost despite knowing exactly where you are? Desperate when you have nothing but excess?

The lonesome poet is a miserable wretch, someone incapable of doing the barest semblance of good despite ample opportunity to do so. They are pretentious while being unimportant, display false piety, and are willing to abandon everything to achieve nothing.

There is as much reason to read their work as there is for them to write it; that is, none at all. If, despite this, you decide you'd like to read some of the work found on this blog, then I hope you find something to laugh at. It might be the only saving grace about this site.

~~CJ~~
