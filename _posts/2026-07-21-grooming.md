---
title: Grooming 
---
As I tug at my facial hair, I wonder how
disillusioned my follicles must feel
knowing that weeks, months of their hard work
is being willingly removed
by their ungrateful host.
Plucked,
I spin the sickle of keratin 
between my fingers,
observing it with the same rigour
I do my own life.
Its tip, frayed.
Yet another thing to worry over.
